Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist


-Do’s
Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts
Think before posting or sharing content
Protect your personal information and privacy
Share informative, positive, and meaningful content
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

-Don’ts
Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content
Avoid sharing fake news or unverified information
Do not overshare personal details like location or contact info
Avoid engaging in arguments or online conflicts
Do not upload inappropriate photos, videos, or language
