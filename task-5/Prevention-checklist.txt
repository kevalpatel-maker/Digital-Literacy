Stay Safe Online – Checklist (India)


-Prevention Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments, especially from emails, WhatsApp, or SMS.
Keep your phone and laptop updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
Only download apps from official stores like Play Store or App Store; avoid cracked or modded apps.
Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or important accounts on unsecured networks.
Review your social media privacy settings and avoid sharing personal details like address, phone number, or college ID.

-UPI & Financial Safety (Important)
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
Always verify payment requests before approving—receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN.
Use only trusted apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm and avoid unknown payment links.

-If Something Goes Wrong
Immediately report cyber fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 as soon as possible for quick action on financial fraud.
